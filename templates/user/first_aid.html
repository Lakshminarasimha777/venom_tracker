Snake-Bite First Aid Guide

⚠️ EMERGENCY
Call the National Poison Control Helpline: 1800-11-6117
Do not delay medical treatment for any reason
Immediate Actions (First 5 Minutes)
  1. Call for Emergency Help

    Call 112 or your local emergency number immediately

  2. Immobilize the Bitten Limb

    Don't move the affected limb. Use a sling or splint if possible

  3. Remove Tight Items

    Remove jewelry, watches, rings from the bitten limb

  4. Keep Person Calm and Still

    Movement increases venom absorption. Keep patient lying down

  5. Apply Pressure Immobilization

    Wrap the limb with elastic bandage (not too tight). Immobilize with splint

❌ DO NOT DO THESE
  • Do NOT cut or suck the wound
  • Do NOT apply ice or tourniquets
  • Do NOT use folk remedies or herbs
  • Do NOT delay going to hospital
  • Do NOT give alcohol or other drugs
⚠️ Warning Symptoms

Seek immediate medical help if you notice:

  • Severe pain or swelling around the bite
  • Discoloration or bruising
  • Bleeding from bite or other sites
  • Difficulty breathing or swallowing
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Drooping eyelids or eye problems
  • Muscle weakness or paralysis
  • Vomiting or abdominal pain
