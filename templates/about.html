About Venom_Tracker

Our Mission

Venom_Tracker is a life-saving emergency network designed to help snake-bite victims find nearby hospitals with anti-venom stock availability. We bridge the gap between patients and medical facilities, ensuring rapid access to life-saving treatment.

The Problem

Snake-bite is a major public health crisis in India. Every year, thousands of lives are lost due to delayed treatment and lack of anti-venom availability. Victims often struggle to find nearby hospitals with the necessary anti-venom stock, costing precious minutes during a medical emergency.

Our Solution

Venom_Tracker provides:

  • Real-time Hospital Location: Find nearby hospitals using GPS
  • Anti-Venom Availability: Check stock levels before heading to a hospital
  • Emergency SOS: Send alerts to multiple hospitals instantly
  • Case Tracking: Monitor your treatment progress
  • First-Aid Guidance: Get immediate guidance while waiting for help

For Hospitals

Hospitals can:

  • Update anti-venom stock in real-time
  • Receive emergency alerts from nearby patients
  • Manage incoming emergency cases
  • Track patient outcomes
  • View analytics and statistics
